Welcome to
DONOSTIA!

Welcome to San Sebastián! San Sebastián, or Donostia in Basque, is a stunning coastal city in the north of Spain, located in the heart of the Basque Country. Known for its breathtaking La Concha Bay, world-renowned gastronomy, and vibrant cultural festivals, San Sebastián is a must-visit destination. It's famous for being the city with the highest concentration of Michelin stars per square meter, as well as hosting the prestigious San Sebastián International Film Festival.
